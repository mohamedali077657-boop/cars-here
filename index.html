About us

Car Bazzar — Driven by passion, built on trust

Car Bazzar is a modern automotive company focused on providing reliable vehicles and exceptional customer experiences. Since our founding, we combine careful engineering, transparent pricing, and a passion for mobility to deliver cars people love to drive.

Our story

2015

Founded as a small local dealership with a clear purpose: make quality cars affordable and reliable.

2018

Expanded to online sales and introduced transparent pricing and simple financing plans.

2022

Launched our certified pre-owned program and opened a state-of-the-art service center.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to make vehicle ownership straightforward, trustworthy, and joy-filled. We envision a future where mobility is accessible to everyone and supported by sustainable practices and smart services.
